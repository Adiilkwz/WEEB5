Frequently Asked Questions

How can I add new friends on Nyam-Nyam?

To add friends, go to the "Friends" page and use the search bar to find users by name or email. Then click the "Add Friend" button.

Can I remove a friend after adding them?

Yes! On the "Friends" page, each friend has a "Delete" button next to their name. Click it to remove them from your list.

Will my friends see my recipes and activities?

Your friends can see your public recipes and interactions, such as likes and comments, depending on your privacy settings.

About Nyam-Nyam

Nyam-Nyam is a food sharing community where users can explore delicious recipes and meet other food lovers.

We aim to connect people who love to cook and discover new dishes. On Nyam-Nyam, you can create your own recipes, follow friends, like and comment on their posts, and explore trending dishes from around the world.
